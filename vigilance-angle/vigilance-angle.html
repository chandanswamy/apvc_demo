GOVERNMENT OF ANDHRA PRADESH

ABSTRACT

Extract from G.O.Ms.No.522, General Administration (Spl.C) Department – issued on 21st July 2007.

G.O.Ms.No. 522

Dated: 21-07-2007

A.P. Vigilance Commission – Scheme defining jurisdiction, powers etc., of Vigilance Commission – Definition of ‘Vigilance angle’ – Incorporated – Orders – Issued.

Read the following:

  1. G.O.Ms.No.421, G.A. (SC.D) Dept., Dated: 03-08-1993.
  2. G.O.Ms.No.147, G.A. (Spl.B) Dept., Dated: 01-05-2000.
ORDER:

In the G.O.s first and second read above, orders were issued defining the jurisdiction and powers of the Vigilance Commission. It has been brought to the notice of the Government that the term ‘Vigilance angle’ has not been comprehensively defined in the Vigilance Manual and that the Central Vigilance Commission has formulated a revised definition of ‘Vigilance angle’.

Government, after careful examination, have decided to redefine the term ‘Vigilance angle’ as formulated by the Central Vigilance Commission and to incorporate the same under Para (6) of the Scheme of Vigilance Commission issued vide G.O. first read above. Accordingly, the following definition shall be incorporated as item (xvii) of Para (6).

Definition of ‘Vigilance Angle’
  1. Demanding or accepting gratification other than legal remuneration in respect of an official act or for using influence with any other official.
  2. Obtaining valuable thing without consideration or with inadequate consideration from persons having or likely to have official dealings.
  3. Obtaining any pecuniary advantage by corrupt or illegal means or abusing official position.
  4. Possession of assets disproportionate to known sources of income.
  5. Cases of misappropriation, forgery, cheating, or similar offences.
  6. Other irregularities implying doubt on integrity, such as gross negligence, reckless decisions, or blatant violations of systems and procedures.
  7. Undue or unjustified delay in disposal of cases that suggests lack of integrity.
  8. Absence of vigilance angle does not exempt from disciplinary action under service rules.

The above definition shall be incorporated under item 1 of Chapter I of the Vigilance Manual, Volume I.

The Secretary to A.P. Vigilance Commission is requested to take necessary action accordingly.

(BY ORDER AND IN THE NAME OF THE GOVERNOR OF ANDHRA PRADESH)

CHITTA RANJAN BISWAL

Principal Secretary to Government (Poll)
